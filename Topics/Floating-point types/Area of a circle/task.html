Area of a circle

Given the radius of a circle, you need to find the area of that circle. Use this formula:

\( S = \pi R^2 \)

where \( S \) is the area of a circle, \( \pi \) is a Math.PI constant, and \( R \) is the radius of the circle.


Sample Input:
100

Sample Output:
31415.926535897932
